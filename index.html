Featured Project

EHR Governance Assistant

The Problem: Enterprise EHR governance involves multiple committees, complex approval workflows, and dozens of stakeholders. People get lost, confused, and frustrated trying to navigate the process.
The Solution: An intuitive process navigator that breaks down each stage of governance into clear, actionable steps. Combined with an AI assistant that answers questions in plain language, it becomes the "easy button" for anyone—from front-line clinicians to executives—to understand where they are and what happens next.

Featured Project

VytalPath Academy

The Problem: New front office healthcare staff—especially career changers from closing factories and other industries—face an overwhelming first day. Medical jargon, insurance workflows, HIPAA compliance, and clinic procedures all at once. The result? Anxiety, costly mistakes, and high turnover in critical roles.
The Solution: A comprehensive training and reference platform built specifically for new clinic staff. 1,000+ healthcare terms in plain language, step-by-step workflows, HIPAA training modules, and smart search that finds answers instantly. They can learn before day one, reference during training, and look things up on the job—all from their phone or desktop.
