My Approach

The CLEAR Framework

A phase-by-phase approach ensuring nothing falls through the cracks—from problem understanding through successful adoption.

C
Clarify

Understand the problem deeply. Review business case, identify stakeholders, assess current state, ensure alignment on success.

L
Lock

Commit to scope and success metrics. Define boundaries, confirm timeline, establish governance, get explicit sign-off.

E
Engineer

Design the solution. Facilitate sessions, create specifications, ensure enterprise standards, document decisions.

A
Assure

Validate through testing. Conduct UAT, support training materials, track defects, confirm go-live readiness.

R
Release

Go live with support. Monitor adoption, address issues quickly, document lessons learned, transition to operations.

The Person

Beyond the resume.

Married 23 years. Two grown sons—ages 20 and 22. My oldest recently married an amazing woman, and they've given me an adorable one-year-old grandson. Yes, I'm a grandma.

I grew up in Santa Cruz, California, riding my beach cruiser around town so carefree that I didn't bother getting my driver's license until I was 18. As a kid, I had an artistic flair—though it took decades for that to show up in my career.

When my kids were young, staying home didn't quite work out—we needed dual income. So I took an evening and weekend job at a local hospital and fell in love with healthcare IT. I knew I wanted more, but nursing wasn't for me, so I earned my Master's in Healthcare Administration.

Through a series of fortunate coincidences—including a Chief Nursing Officer asking me to help with an EMR update—I landed my first analyst role. The rest is history. I've been in healthcare IT ever since.

More recently, learning to leverage AI and build my own custom solutions has brought back that artistic flair. I love being able to work the entire process from beginning to end—seeing and diagnosing a problem, then architecting and building the solution myself. It's quite satisfying.

About ten years ago, we left Santa Cruz for the foothills of California's Gold Country, near Jackson. My husband and I love the country life—gardening, hanging out with my chickens, and enjoying the peace and quiet. My neighbor across the street has beautiful horses I get to admire every day.
